Job Title:Freelance Artist

We are looking for some new talented artists!

Job details

Is Lofi Girl your go-to music channel for studying, drawing, chilling, or sleeping? Do you take great interest in the team behind the radios, compilations, etc., and how they help set tones and tell stories along with the music? We are now giving you the opportunity to get involved directly behind-the-scenes in creating stunning and innovative visuals for our upcoming projects to share with the world! Our team is very passionate about what we do, and our utmost goal is to share our love for lofi and relaxing music with the world, through our dreamy visuals and animations.

Job brief

As a Lofi Girl freelancer, you will be working closely with the rest of the creative team to create immersive and breathtaking illustrations, animations, or assets for our music, website, and many more projects to come. Be the first to know about upcoming projects and new additions to the Lofi Girl universe, and help bring them to life!

  • Collaborate closely with and take direction from our artistic director.

  • Implement changes and completely finished works in accordance with schedules.

  • Maintain an open and professional demeanor regarding direction, changes, and shifting requirements.
