Get to know us

Founded in early 2022, Lofi Studio is a dedicated group of individuals who are committed to creating visually stunning works. Initially, the team worked on album and EP covers for Lofi Records and later developed animations and visuals for the Lofi Girl compilation. As the team expanded, we decided to broaden our scope and establish the studio. Lofi Studio is now home to a diverse group of illustrators, animators, and designers from around the globe, all united by a shared passion for creating meaningful and beautiful art. In addition to working with well-known brands such as Disney and Psyonix, we are able to adapt to a variety of styles and cater to a range of needs. Our art director carefully selects our illustrators and animators to ensure that you can trust the team you are working with.
